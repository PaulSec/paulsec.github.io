Connecting Meross MSG100 to TUBAUTO ProCom 7-3 Garage Door: A Quick Guide

Modernizing your garage door to be smart-enabled can greatly enhance convenience and security. The Meross MSG100 Smart Wi-Fi Garage Door Opener offers an easy solution to connect your garage door to your home network, allowing you to control it remotely through the Meross application. If you have a TUBAUTO ProCom 7-3 garage door opener, integrating it with the Meross MSG100 is straightforward with the right guidance.

Step-by-Step Guide:

  1. Review the Manuals: Before starting the installation process, it’s crucial to familiarize yourself with both the Meross MSG100 and the TUBAUTO ProCom 7-3 manuals (you can find the french version here: https://www.tubauto.fr/app/uploads/2018/02/procom7-3-fr.pdf). Understanding the wiring and setup requirements outlined in the manuals ensures a smooth installation process.

  2. Identify Connection Points: Refer to the TUBAUTO ProCom 7-3 manual, specifically focusing on Figure 11 on page 25. This diagram provides a visual representation of the connection points required for integrating external devices like the Meross MSG100.

  3. Disconnect Power: With the power switched off, unplug the TUBAUTO ProCom 7-3 garage door opener from its power source. This ensures complete safety while working with electrical components.

  4. Connect Cables: Following the guidelines provided in the Meross MSG100 manual and Figure 11 of the TUBAUTO ProCom 7-3 manual, carefully connect the cables from the Meross device to the designated connection points on the garage door opener. Ensure proper insulation and secure connections to prevent any electrical mishaps.

  1. Power On: Once the cables are securely connected, restore power to the TUBAUTO ProCom 7-3 garage door opener by plugging it back into the power source.

  2. Test the Connection: With the Meross MSG100 now connected to the TUBAUTO ProCom 7-3 garage door opener, test the functionality by using the Meross app to open and close the garage door remotely. Verify that the commands are executed correctly and that the integration is successful.

  3. Final Checks: After successful integration, perform a final inspection to ensure all connections are secure and there are no loose cables. Double-check that the garage door operates smoothly both manually and through the app.

By following these simple steps and referring to the provided manuals, you can easily connect your TUBAUTO ProCom 7-3 garage door opener to the Meross MSG100, transforming your garage into a smart and connected space. Enjoy!
